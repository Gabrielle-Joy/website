Quiet Meditations

The thoughts as they come

The thoughts as they go

Will be written here

In the words that I know.

Perseverance and consistency beats giving up every time

The more we fail the harder it is to try again. But the more we fail the closer we are to success.

Slow down and reflect.

Be self-aware. Know what you believe in and why.

Know your worth

Sometimes life comes at us in ways we do not expect.

But if we have a limited view of the world around us,

then our expectations are also limited.

Gabrielle Joy
